About Codec

For the past 2 years, Codec has been producing visual merchandising display tools and windows for the luxury and fashion sectors. With an influential list of international clients, Codec’s unique approach in tackling production challenges continues to inspire clients around the world, and influence production partners working with us.

Codec’s dedication to achieving workable solutions beyond its clients' imagination is what sets it apart. Today, Codec’s value is reflected in its ability to tackle the task of blending sophisticated engineering with its clients’ modern aesthetics. With over 40 different projects under its belt, Codec has manufactured over 50,000 pieces of goods for many prestigious brands around the world.
